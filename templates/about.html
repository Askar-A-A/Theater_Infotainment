Welcome to Wiener Staatsoper

Wiener Staatsoper is one of the leading opera houses in the world. Its past is steeped in tradition. Its present is alive with richly varied performances and events. Each season, the schedule features 350 performances of more than 60 different operas and ballets.

Our commitment to excellence in opera and classical music has made us a cornerstone of Vienna's rich cultural heritage and a beacon of artistic achievement worldwide.

Our Rich History

1869
Opening of the Vienna Court Opera with Mozart's Don Juan in the presence of Emperor Franz Joseph and Empress Elisabeth
1897
Gustav Mahler becomes director of the Vienna Court Opera, ushering in a new golden age
1945
Reopening after World War II with Mozart's Figaro under Josef Krips
1969
Centennial celebration with a new production of Richard Strauss's Der Rosenkavalier
