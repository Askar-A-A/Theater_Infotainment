{% placeholder "question_3" or %}Can I also watch performances of Wiener Staatsoper live at home?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_3" or %}Yes, selected performances are available for streaming through our digital platform. Visit our streaming service website for more information.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_4" or %}Which benefits do I enjoy as a partner of Wiener Staatsoper?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_4" or %}Partners enjoy various benefits including priority booking, exclusive events, backstage tours, and special discounts. Contact our partnership office for detailed information.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_5" or %}What is the dress code for performances?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_5" or %}While there is no strict dress code, evening wear is customary and appreciated. Many guests choose formal attire: suits or tuxedos for gentlemen and evening dresses or elegant pantsuits for ladies. We recommend avoiding overly casual clothing out of respect for the performers and other guests.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_6" or %}Are there subtitles available during performances?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_6" or %}Yes, all performances feature digital subtitles in multiple languages. These are displayed on small screens on the back of each seat or above the stage. Languages typically include German and English, with additional languages available for selected performances.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_7" or %}How early should I arrive before the performance?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_7" or %}We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the performance begins. This allows time for parking, coat check, finding your seat, and settling in. The doors typically open 60 minutes before curtain time. Please note that latecomers may not be admitted until a suitable break in the performance.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_8" or %}Is photography allowed during performances?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_8" or %}Photography, video recording, and audio recording are strictly prohibited during all performances. This includes the use of mobile phones and other electronic devices. Photos may be taken in the foyer and other public areas before the performance and during intermissions.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_9" or %}Are there facilities for disabled visitors?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_9" or %}Yes, our theater is fully accessible to disabled visitors. We offer wheelchair spaces in the auditorium, accessible restrooms, and elevator access to all levels. Please inform our box office of any special requirements when booking your tickets so we can ensure appropriate arrangements are made.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_10" or %}Is there parking available at the theater?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_10" or %}While the theater does not have its own parking facility, there are several public parking garages within walking distance. We recommend the Opera Garage or City Parking. Special evening rates may apply during performance times. Valet parking service is also available for an additional fee.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_11" or %}Are refreshments available?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_11" or %}Yes, our theater features several bars and refreshment areas where you can purchase drinks and light snacks before the performance and during intermissions. We recommend pre-ordering your intermission refreshments to avoid queuing. Please note that food and drinks are not permitted in the auditorium.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_12" or %}What happens if I can't attend a performance I've booked?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_12" or %}Tickets cannot be refunded but may be exchanged for another performance (subject to availability) or returned for resale. Exchange fees may apply. Please contact our box office as soon as possible if you cannot attend. Season ticket holders have additional flexibility regarding exchanges.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_13" or %}Do you offer guided tours of the theater?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_13" or %}Yes, we offer guided tours of our historic theater on select days. Tours typically include the auditorium, foyer, and sometimes backstage areas when available. Please check our website or contact the box office for the current tour schedule and booking information.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_14" or %}Is there a dress rehearsal I can attend?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_14" or %}Select dress rehearsals are occasionally open to the public, typically for educational purposes or for special membership programs. Information about open rehearsals will be posted on our website and social media channels when available.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_15" or %}Are there discounts available for students or seniors?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_15" or %}Yes, we offer discounted tickets for students, seniors, and young people under 26. Valid ID must be presented when purchasing discounted tickets and upon entry to the performance. There are also special last-minute ticket options for students.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_16" or %}How can I purchase tickets?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_16" or %}Tickets can be purchased online through our official website, by phone, or in person at our box office. We recommend booking in advance for popular performances as they often sell out quickly. The box office is open Monday through Saturday from 10am to 6pm, and until curtain time on performance days.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_17" or %}Is there a cloakroom service?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_17" or %}Yes, we provide a complimentary cloakroom service for coats, large bags, and umbrellas. The cloakroom is located in the main foyer and opens one hour before performances. For security reasons, large bags and backpacks must be checked at the cloakroom.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_18" or %}Can I bring children to performances?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_18" or %}Children are welcome at our theater, but please note that most standard opera performances are recommended for children ages 8 and older. We offer special family performances and children's programs throughout the season that are designed specifically for younger audiences. Children must have their own ticket regardless of age.{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "question_19" or %}How long are performances typically?{% endplaceholder %}
{% placeholder "answer_19" or %}Performance lengths vary considerably depending on the opera. Most performances range from 2 to 3.5 hours, including intermissions. The specific running time for each performance is listed on our website and in the program. Intermissions typically last 20-25 minutes.{% endplaceholder %}
